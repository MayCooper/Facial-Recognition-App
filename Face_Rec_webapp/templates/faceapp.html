Facial Recognition Application


Gender Classification:

This application made by May Cooper harnesses the power of data and a sophisticated machine learning model. When a user uploads an image, the app performs a series of operations in the background.

It converts the image to grayscale, crops the face region, transforms it into an eigen image, and utilizes a trained model for prediction. All of these functionalities are seamlessly integrated into a Flask app, ensuring a smooth and user-friendly experience.

The app's ability to accurately classify gender is a result of the advanced techniques employed behind the scenes, showcasing the potential of machine learning in real-world applications.
